Corporate Giving

StalwartMC employees have nominated five Houston non-profit organizations that share our Values of Stewardship and Respect. Each organization will receive a financial gift on behalf of StalwartMC employees.

However, we want your help deciding three finalists. Read about each organization and cast a vote, indicating which one you’d like us champion as a Stalwart Gives recipient.  The top three vote recipients will receive financial support through our 2018 Corporate Giving Program and the highest vote tally will be the non-profit partner for the 2018 StalwartMC Community Day of Service.  Cast your vote today!
