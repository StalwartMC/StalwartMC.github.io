Gambit Construction and StalwartMC: Partnering for the Future

November 27, 2017

Two local companies, Gambit Construction (Cypress, TX) and Stalwart Consulting, LLC. (Katy, TX) are coming together to announce its services agreement. Due to an increasing recruiting demand for Project Managers and Estimators, Gambit Construction engaged StalwartMC to analyze and improve its business processes, allowing for continued growth while maintaining the highest levels of client and customer satisfaction. This strategic talent initiative aims to match high quality candidates in the market with Gambit’s culture of excellence in the multi-family renovation industry.

"We are excited to partner with a leading business strategy and technical implementation firm, StalwartMC, “says Gambit President, Ronnie Rabbitt. “We believe that, combined with our current talent in these positions and future potential hires, Gambit Construction will continue to be at the forefront of our industry in Houston. StalwartMC brings the experience and expertise of a large consulting firm; they’ll provide us with a much-needed blueprint for strengthening how we do recruiting."

Gambit Construction is a leading full-service general contractor in the Houston Texas Metro area with deep experience handling a wide range of projects from full scale renovations to minor repairs in the apartment industry. ​From Cypress to Sugarland, Baytown to the Woodlands, Gambit completes multi- family projects on-time with the utmost attention to detail at a competitive price.

StalwartMC is an innovative management consulting firm based in Katy, Texas. While standing behind its core values of operating with integrity and delivering excellence, StalwartMC was founded on the basis of helping others achieve more. Its focus on Business Strategy and Technology Services lends itself to serve businesses in and around the Houston community and beyond.
